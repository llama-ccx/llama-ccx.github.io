    Buy a License¶

    Llama offers a free tier for models with up to 100 elements. For larger models, a license is required.

    License Plans¶

    Academic License — Free¶

    For students, researchers, and educators. Valid for 1 year, renewable upon request.

    • University coursework and teaching
    • Academic research and publications
    • Personal learning and experimentation

    Not for commercial use

    The Academic License does not cover professional or commercial work. See the License terms for details.

    To request an Academic License, send an email to llama.calculix@gmail.com with:

    • Your full name
    • University or institution name
    • Academic email address

    You will receive a license file (data.bin) within 48 hours.

    Commercial License — 47 € + VAT / year¶

    For engineering firms, consultancies, freelancers, and any commercial use.

    • Per-seat license, valid for 1 year
    • Access to all versions released within the maintenance period
    • Email support with a maximum 48-hour response time

    After Payment¶

    Once payment is confirmed, you will receive a license file (data.bin) by email within 48 hours. To activate it:

    1. Open Grasshopper
    2. Go to Llama → License → Activate License
    3. Select the data.bin file you received

    Invoices¶

    If you require a quotation, proforma invoice, or your company's details on the invoice, email llama.calculix@gmail.com before paying. Otherwise, you will receive an invoice with the name and email address associated with your payment.
